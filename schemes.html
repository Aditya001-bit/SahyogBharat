Housing

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Gramin (PMAY-G)

Scheme to provide pucca houses with basic amenities to all rural houseless households and those living in kutcha houses. ₹1.2 lakh assistance per house and targets 2.95 crore houses.

₹1.2 Lakh Assistance 2.95 Crore Houses
